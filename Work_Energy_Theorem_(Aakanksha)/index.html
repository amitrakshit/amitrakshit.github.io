Solution

•  The work done is equal to the change      in the kinetic energy.

•  The velocity of the car changes from          5 m/s to 10 m/s.

•  The kinetic energy of the car changes     from 1/2 × m × 5² to 1/2 × m × 10².

•  The work done by the engine will be     change in K.E i.e.
    1/2 × m × (10² - 5²) = 75/2 × m.

You just explored that according to the work-energy theorem, the work done is equal to the change in the kinetic energy.
